Beyond paintings...


Lucia does not only have a passion for the narrow art of oil on canvas, but it spills over into other categories such as bags, accessories, jewelry, bijoux and workshops.

Bags and accessories


Bags, belts, shoes: do you own an accessory you wish to creatively renew or are you looking for an original item?

Jewelry and bijoux


In collaboration with master goldsmiths and artisans and creatives of the sector I make jewels and bijoux, even on commission.

Workshop & courses


Do you want to learn creative makeover techniques or find out how many new lives your objects can have?

Restoration


Old frames, trays and other small homeware needing new care may become true masterpieces or brand new objects, fun and precious. Find out all the possibilities of a new look for the things of your home.

Creative styling


It may be that you are thinking of a way to renew and reinvent the look of your outdoor furniture to create a colorful and fun atmosphere for your mornings of evenings in the garden. Contact us to get a quote.

Interior and design


Do you want to freshen up your old porcelain, redo the living room or turn your wardrobe into a piece of art?
