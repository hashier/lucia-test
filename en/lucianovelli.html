The life and work of Lucia Novelli

Biography

Colors, Cats & Coffee

Born in Pescara, a charming small city on the Italian riviera, she grew up in the vibrant, international scene of Milan. She studied at the renowned Academy of Arts Accademia di Brera in Milan and at the Università degli Studi di Parma, Course in Artistic Heritage Preservation, alongside other educational courses in graphics, decoration, visual communication and textile design.

Between 1980 and 1982, she collaborated as a graphic designer and illustrator for important brands such as Pierrel, FM Comunicazioni and Mondadori. Since 1984, she collaborated in the creation of collections for fashion brands including Zucchi, Magnolia, Malerba, Pink Drake, Benetton and Bellia, among others.

During the past 23 years, she has focused on painting and the artistic restoration of furniture. Her works are present in both public and private collections.
