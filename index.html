The Artist

Surrealism, Fantasy & Love

The unique blend of surrealism, fantasy and love comes together in a colorful and vivid way that puts Lucia's personal touch on every painting.

Born in Pescara and raised in the vibrant international scene of Milan, Lucia studied at the renowned Accademia di Brera and has been dedicated to painting for over 23 years.
